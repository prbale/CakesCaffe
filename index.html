Frequently asked questions

  • Is a deposit required when placing an order?

    • We require at least half of the total as the deposit.

  • Can I make changes to my cake after I order?

    • You can make changes a week before your delivery/pick up date. Changes can be made by calling us at 8530669827

  • Refund/Exchange Policy

    • We take pride in our desserts and cakes. If you are unhappy with your order and/or purchase, immediately notify us (within 24 hours ) of your concern We retain the right to judge our product quality for ourselves & determine the amount that can be refunded. All refunds will be provided as in-store credit.

  • Do you make cakes for people with allergies?

    • We make egg free, nut free, dairy free, & gluten free cakes. These orders need to be placed at least 1 week in advance. We do not make vegan cakes. We can only accommodate one allergy per cake with the only exception being nut free.
